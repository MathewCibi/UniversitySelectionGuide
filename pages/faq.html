FAQs

How can I get in touch with universities for further inquiries?

To get in touch with universities for further inquiries, you can easily find their contact information on our website. Simply navigate to the specific university's page you are interested in, and you will find a link to the official university website. On their website, you will find comprehensive contact details, including phone numbers, email addresses, and sometimes even live chat options. Feel free to reach out to the university directly with any questions or concerns you may have. They will be more than happy to assist you and provide you with the information you need to make an informed decision about your educational journey.

Is your website only focused on domestic universities?

Currently, our website primarily focuses on providing information about universities in New Zealand. However, we have plans to expand our coverage to include international universities in the future. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive resource for students, offering insights into both domestic and international educational opportunities. Stay tuned for updates as we continue to enhance our website and broaden our range of universities to help you explore study options worldwide.

Is there going to be more universities?

Yes, we are continuously updating and expanding our university selection guide to provide you with the most comprehensive and up-to-date information. We understand that there are many universities around the world, and our goal is to include as many relevant institutions as possible. We are actively researching and adding new universities to our database to ensure that you have access to a wide range of options for your academic journey. Keep checking back regularly for updates, as we strive to make our selection guide as inclusive and informative as possible.
