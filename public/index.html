This is the University of Auckland, a world-class institution located in the vibrant city of Auckland, New Zealand. Established in 1883, the university is the largest and highest-ranked university in New Zealand and is consistently ranked in the top 100 universities worldwide.


With over 40,000 students, including 8,000 international students from more than 120 countries, the University of Auckland offers a diverse and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, research innovation, and cultural understanding.


The university is home to eight faculties, which offer a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in various fields such as Arts, Business, Law, Engineering, Medicine, Sciences, and more. Students benefit from world-class facilities, including modern classrooms, cutting-edge laboratories, and state-of-the-art research centres.
